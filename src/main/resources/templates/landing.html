What does it mean to be Tier-1 Certified?

Job Seekers

Save time by creating one universal job application portfolio. Upload and verify credentials and transcripts, pre-screen background checks for hiring managers and recruiters, being Tier One certified helps you take care of the important things instead of spending hours applying for new jobs. 

Companies

Save time by filtering straight to candidates who meet you qualifications. Saving time vetting resumes and screening candidates who may not meet company expectations or qualifications. Search our library of candidates who already have background checks and verified education and credentials.
